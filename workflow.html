WORK FLOW









Methodology

In this project we are using the “Applied” methodology to carry out our research. We used the knowledge that is generated from other basic types of research and we are trying to find solutions for a practical problem that exists in the land registry in Sri Lanka. This research is going to use “Blockchain Technology” and we decided to use the Consensus algorithm and smart contract to validate and develop our system.


In our application we have divided our dashboard into major two parts . They are User dashboard and Legal Officer dashboard. In the user dashboard the user can act as seller or buyer. As a seller he can add new land to sell and can view the land requests which are sent by the buyer. And as a Buyer he can view their buyer dashboard , and can request the available lands which are updated by the Seller. In the Legal officer dashboard, legal officers can verify the user and land. As well as a legal officer able to view land records and transactions details, he is the person who will run the smart contract to transfer the Land ownership.


The main stakeholders elaborate in our Blockchain Land Registry Platform:


• Buyer: A person who likes to buy the land will use the platform to find the property, request access, and link with the seller and will get the land title ownership.

• Owner (Seller): A person who wishes to sell the land and will use the platform to supervise property details and hand over the land title to buyers.

• Legal officer: A person who will audit land transferring procedure and behave as a legitimate body below whose supervision this buying and selling are done.


Product Workflow

Registration of users and property

  • To register on the platform, users and legal officers need to visit the application and need to make registration. On clicking the submit button, the user will get verified through email and from there, the user will be redirected to the login page.

  • Login, users must provide their Username and Password.

  • To register the property, choose the Add Land tab from the dashboard and fill all the details related to the property and owner. After submitting the form, the user's application is then verified by the government authority(Legal officer).


Validation from government authority

  • This step, government authority (Legal Officer) audits the user's land details and has the right to approve or decline their application.

  • During the time of Registration of land, users have to upload the legal documents of land so that the government and the buyer can verify it. If rejected, the user has to submit a new application, and it cannot be available for other users to buy. And if it gets accepted, then the owner of the land has the option to make their land available to sell.

  • When Government accepts or rejects the request, it will notify the user in the user's dashboard.


Transaction between both the parties

This step has several stages involved. Following are the steps required:-

  • Making the land available Once the Government is approving it, the landowner has the option to make their land available to other users.

  • requests to the landowner to purchase When the land is available to buy, the buyer moves toward their dashboard, and in the Buy land section, they search for the property and send a request to the landowner to buy it.

  • Viewing the request, the Land proprietor views the buyer's requests and after communicating with them, plans whether to allow them to buy the land or not. They have the option to accept or decline the request.

  • Processing the request Once the landowner views the requester address and if it is found to be the right one, then the seller accepts the request.

  • Buying the property Once the request is approved, the buyer can now buy the property. Once the seller accepts the buyer’s request, the legal officer will be notified. A legal officer is the person who needs to transfer the ownership of the previous landowner to the new owner. This ownership transaction will be run through a smart contract.

    • The transaction process is conducted in the form of the smart contract, which ensures that the process is immutable, secured and d igitized. No one in between can tamper the data, and authenticity is maintained throughout the process. There is no human error involved. Paperwork will be reduced, and most importantly, everything will become transparent. Chances of fraud cases will be reduced. The public ledger can be used in case there is any doubt regarding the land ownership claim.

Findings

Blockchain technology has impacted the real estate industry in a variety of ways, including offering a new means for buyers and sellers to connect with one another. Blockchain could be used to cut intermediaries out of the real estate transaction process, thereby reducing costs.
