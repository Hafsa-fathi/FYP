Ethereum Test Networks

Developers and enterprises can use test networks to evaluate their business logic by deploying smart contracts before releasing their product and services to the Ethereum main-net. The test-nets offer the same service without the need to transfer monetary tokens. We'll go over the top three test-nets, which are as follows:

  • Ropsten : Test-net in Ropsten, and its Ether is mined in the same way as the main-net. Ropsten is the most similar to the current main-net due to its PoW consensus algorithm, which is the same as the real main-net.

  • Kovan: Unlike the main network, Kovan only supports the Parity client and uses PoA as the consensus algorithm. As a result, Kovan cannot be considered an effective accurate simulation of the current main-net. Despite this, it is resistant to spam attacks, reliable, and stable, making it suitable for public testing.

  • Rinkeby: Rinkeby has the same benefits as Kovan, but only supports the Geth client
