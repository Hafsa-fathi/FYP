Ethereum Wallet

Ether is Ethereum's native currency, and users frequently manage their Ether in software wallets which are implemented as browser extensions. These extensions get the user's private key and provide a graphical user interface for managing the user's funds. MetaMask is the most popular in-browser wallet as of September 2021. Metamask is a Chrome extension that simplifies interactions with Ethereum networks. Metamask not only acts as a wallet that shows the history of all the transactions that took place on blockchain but also helps in account management.
