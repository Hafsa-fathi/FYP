ABOUT US









Team

We are team of undergraduates of the Faculty of technology, University of Sri jayewardenepura came up with the solution to the manual land registration. As a solution we developed a blockchain based web application for land registration. With blockchain, real estate transactions can be completed faster and with less paperwork. This is because blockchain allows for the digital transfer of assets, so there is no need for paper contracts or other physical documentation. Everything can be done electronically, which can save a lot of time and is cost effective.

The moment of found research idea

We found our research idea while having discussion with our Supervisors. While we are thinking about current issues in Sri Lanka, we suddenly blink with manual land registration issues. However manual land registration faces several issues, we come across to solve this problem with blockchain technology.

Why we attach ourselves with this research ?

Since the Blockchain technology has impacted the real estate industry in a variety of ways, including offering a new means for buyers and sellers to connect with one another. Blockchain could be used to cut intermediaries out of the real estate transaction process, thereby reducing costs.

The Blockchain in the land registry is used for secure transfer of land property. The transparent nature of Blockchain enables it to track the changes made in land documents. Advent of Blockchain technology in the land registry is playing a very beneficial role in this developing era. Thereby we unquestionably connected with our project.
